The Game

Dradyn: Untold

A party based fantasy action RPG set inside the larger World of Dradyn.

View Classes Follow Development

Game Overview

Dradyn: Untold focuses on exploration, action combat, story-driven companions, and the slow discovery of hidden forces moving through the world.

Exploration

Travel Across Dradyn

Explore a world divided by the River Ahmn, spanning towns, wild frontiers, and forgotten ruins. Overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and use your abilities to carve a path forward.

Party

Fight Alongside Your Companions

Travel with your two closest allies, joined at times by story-driven companions unique to each region of Dradyn.

Combat

Action RPG Battles

Fight with responsive movement, abilities, class identity, and tactical party support.

Party & Multiplayer

Travel with your two closest friends and a host of other characters as you traverse the world of Dradyn. Companions are tied to the story and the regions you explore, giving the party a strong narrative role without turning the game into a roster collector.

Couch Co-Op

Share the Story

Up to three other players can join you locally and control party members while you play through the story together.

Online Multiplayer

Drop Into Another Adventure

Up to three other players can bring their own main character into your world, earning experience, drops, and gold without changing their own story progress.

Beyond the Main Story

After completing the main story, players can keep testing their builds, party strategies, and teamwork through harder content and expanded progression.

New Game+

So You Want to Be a Hero

Replay the story at increased difficulty with a raised level cap up to 70.

Mercenaries

Round Out Your Party

Hire mercenaries after the main story to support more flexible solo and multiplayer party builds.

Endless Dungeons

Push Deeper

Enter escalating dungeons with tougher encounters and greater loot rewards.
