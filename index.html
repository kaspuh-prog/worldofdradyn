What is Dradyn

Dradyn is a living fantasy world shaped by decades of storytelling. Heroes rise and fall, ancient forces stir beneath the surface, and the people who live there rarely comprehend the complexity of conflicts happening all around them.

Some stories are told through the lives of characters. Others unfold through the choices made in game. Together, they reveal a world that is larger than any single perspective.

Two Ways to Experience Dradyn

The Game

Dradyn: Untold

A party based fantasy action RPG focused on exploration, combat, and discovery. Travel across the world, build your party, and uncover the forces shaping Dradyn from behind the scenes.

The Story

Tales from Dradyn

A series of novellas released chapter by chapter. Follow characters living within the world as their paths cross events that shape the future of Dradyn.

The World

The River Ahmn, also called the Ahmn Belt, stretches east and west across Dradyn, dividing the northern kingdoms from the southern wilds. Above it stand floating Langtree, riverbound Telmbrook, holy Aspil, mountainlocked Thrombeldo, and the hidden forests of Silvaar.

South of the river, the lands open into ranch country, plains, desert, pirate strongholds, sacred forest enclaves, and the southern coast.
